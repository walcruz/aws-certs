Spot: 2min of grace period
Spot Block: 1 to 6 hours period without interruptions

Spot Fleets: set of Spot instances + (optional) On-demand
- Try to meet target capacity with price
- Strategies:
    - lowestPrice (popular)
    - diversified - availability and long workload
    - capacityOptimized - optimal capacity for the number of instances
    - priceCapacityOptimized (recommended) - pools with the maximun capacity them lowest price. (best choice for the most workloads)
